How do I get HBO on my TV?

There are a couple of ways to watch HBO on your TV (and the rest of your devices). Here’s the breakdown:


Sign up for Max

Max is the enhanced service featuring all of HBO. With Max, you can stream your favorite award-winning HBO series, blockbuster movies, and rewatch-worthy specials. There’s something to enjoy for every mood, with streamable options spanning a wide range of genres, including comedy, drama, reality, lifestyle, food, true crime, and more.

And good news, you might already have access to Max. HBO Max subscribers automatically get Max, so you won’t miss a beat with your streaming. If you’re a new subscriber, you can visit Max.com to sign up.

Learn more about Max here.


Get HBO through your TV provider

Alternatively, you can subscribe to HBO through your television provider. This subscription will grant you access to the HBO television channels (there are seven 24-hour HBO networks on traditional pay television), which you can check the schedule for here. Plus, you’ll be able to use your account details to get Max, too. To learn more, contact your provider.
