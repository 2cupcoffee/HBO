Celtics City

1 Seasons | 3 Episodes | TV-14
Watch Now

Celtics City chronicles the remarkable saga of the Boston Celtics, the NBA’s winningest and most storied franchise. From the team's founding as one of the league’s original members to its triumphant 2024 championship, the documentary series delves into the fierce rivalries, defining moments, and societal forces that have shaped the organization’s enduring legacy.

HBO Sports Documentaries, Ringer Films, and Words + Pictures present Celtics City. Director, Lauren Stowell; executive producers, Bill Simmons, Connor Schell, Aaron Cohen, Libby Geist, Wyc Grousbeck, and Rich Gotham; co-executive producer and showrunner, Gabe Honig; produced by Sascha Gardner and Christina Lenis; consulting producer, Jackie MacMullan. For HBO: executive producers, Nancy Abraham, Lisa Heller, and Bentley Weiner; supervising producer, Abtin Motia. 


1.Chapter I: Founding Fathers

Led by Bob Cousy and Bill Russell, the Celtics become an NBA dynasty, but racial tensions in Boston and beyond augur a turbulent era ahead.

2.Chapter II: No Final Victories

Amid backlash for his commitment to civil rights, Bill Russell makes history as the first Black head coach in professional sports.

3.Chapter III: All Swept Up

The Celtics win a pair of titles in the '70s amid racial unrest, and near the end of the decade, they draft Indiana State star Larry Bird.

4.Chapter IV: Great Hope. Period.

Larry Bird elevates the Celtics and struggling NBA to new heights, but internal discord threatens to derail another potential dynasty.

6.Chapter VI: Untenable Toll

The tragic death of Len Bias casts a shadow over the Celtics. In 1992, Larry Bird’s retirement closes a chapter for the team and the NBA.
